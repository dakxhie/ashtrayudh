  • Tasks you create, edit, boost, recharge, delete, or complete.

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with service providers needed to operate VORTIX, comply with law, protect users, prevent fraud, or enforce our policies.

7. Data Retention

You may request deletion of your account and related personal data by contacting us. Some records may be retained where necessary for safety, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, legal compliance, or platform integrity.

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.
