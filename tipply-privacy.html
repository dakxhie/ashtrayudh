Privacy Policy — Tipply

Last updated: November 2025

Welcome to Tipply — a platform by Ashtrayudh that lets users discover, engage, and promote creative content through a coin-based reward system.

1. Information We Collect

Tipply collects limited information necessary to provide a secure and fair user experience. This includes:

  • Email address (for login and account management)
  • Basic engagement data (content viewed, coins earned/spent)
  • Device and app version details (for performance and crash analytics)

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used to:

  • Authenticate users and manage accounts
  • Track engagement and coin balance
  • Maintain app security and prevent misuse
  • Improve user experience and fix technical issues

3. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell, trade, or rent users’ personal information. Data may be securely processed by trusted third-party services such as:

  • Google Firebase — for authentication, analytics, and crash reporting
  • AdMob — for rewarded ads shown voluntarily by users

All data is encrypted and transmitted securely using industry-standard methods.

4. Ads and Monetization

Tipply may display rewarded video ads via Google AdMob. These ads are shown only when users choose to view them. No sensitive personal data is used to target ads.

5. User Control and Data Deletion

Users can request account deletion or data removal at any time by contacting our support email below. Once deleted, your account and associated data cannot be recovered.

6. Children’s Privacy

Tipply is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 years of age.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect improvements or legal requirements. The latest version will always be available at this page.
