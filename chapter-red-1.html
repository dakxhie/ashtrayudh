Chapter 1 — The Awakening

The adventure begins with an unexpected event that changes everything for our protagonist. The town was quiet, almost too quiet, until a sudden scream pierced the dawn.

Shadows moved along the cobblestone streets, and a sense of unease settled over the familiar town square. This was no ordinary morning, and our hero could feel that something momentous was about to unfold.

Every decision, every encounter, and every step would lead toward a path that no one could have predicted. The journey of discovery had begun.
